Tips for Preparing Grocery Shopping on a Budget

Always go with a list

  • Do not shop when your hungry!
  • Stick with your list and don't do impulse buying!
  • Check you pantry for stock!

Plan your meals

  • Keep it simple!
  • Make another meals out of leftovers!
  • Ask your family for suggestions!

Clip Coupons

  • Watch for expired dates on your coupons!
  • Do not over buy items you don't usually purchase!

Buy fresh fruit & vegetables

  • Fresh produce is better than juices and fruit snacks.
  • Check skin for ripeness, but avoid produce that is wrinkled or wilted.

Buy in season!

  • Early birds gets the fresh product!
